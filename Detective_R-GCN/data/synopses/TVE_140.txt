--- NOTES ON THE SERIES ---

Shetland Series 5 is one of the most emotionally resonant entries in the series,
using the accumulated weight of four previous series to give the investigation
and its personal dimensions a depth that earlier series were still building
toward. The Shetland community — rendered across five series with real love
and real knowledge — is the show's greatest achievement, and Series 5 is the
series that most fully honours what it means to investigate crime in a place
you love among people you know. Douglas Henshall's Perez is one of British
crime drama's finest detective creations, and Series 5 is among his finest
work in the role.
